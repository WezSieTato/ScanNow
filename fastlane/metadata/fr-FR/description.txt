ScanNow is a simple and fast application for scanning documents on iOS. With ScanNow, you can scan, save and share any document in seconds. ScanNow uses your iPhone's camera to take photos of your documents and converts them into images or PDFs. You can then share your scanned documents by email, SMS, or save them on your device for later use. ScanNow is perfect for scanning receipts, notes, invoices, business cards, certificates, etc. Download ScanNow today and start scanning!
